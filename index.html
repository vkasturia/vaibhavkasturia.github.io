<div class="container flow-text">
<blockquote>
<h2>Data Scientist</h2>
</blockquote>

<p>I am working as a Senior Data Scientist at <a href="https://www.continental.com/" target="_blank">Continental AG</a> Germany. Currently, I am the product owner of Demand Forecasting Platform for Supply chain management. I am leading the development of this platform which includes adding new data sources, adding new machine learning models, feature engineering etc.</p>
<p>I personally like to work in an entrepreneurial environment with the freedom of working on new ideas which has potential to create new business and significant impact on society. In Germany, I do not see that kind of mindset especially in major companies but you think you are different then I will be happy to have a chat.</p>
<p>Being inspired from <a href="https://www.ted.com" target="_blank">TED</a>, I also organise TEDx events at <a href="https://www.tedxhannover.de" target="_blank">TEDxHannover</a> in the beautiful city of <a href="https://www.hannover.de" target="_blank">Hannover</a>.</p>
